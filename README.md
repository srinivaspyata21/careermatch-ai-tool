# CareerMatch AI — Free Job Search Toolkit

> **Free. No sign-up. No cost. Works in any browser.**

A complete job search toolkit built in a single HTML file. ATS keyword scorer, salary benchmarks, interview prep guides, AI prompt templates, and job portal directory — covering **16 industries** across Canada and globally.

**[🚀 Live Demo →](https://srinivaspyata21.github.io/careermatch-ai-tool)**

---

## What It Does

Most job seekers apply with the same generic resume to every role and wonder why they don't hear back. This toolkit fixes that. It gives you the tools professionals use — ATS optimization, salary data, interview frameworks — completely free, with no account required.

---

## Features

### 🎯 ATS Keyword Scorer
Paste any job description, select your industry, and instantly see:
- Your estimated keyword match score (0–100%)
- Which keywords from your profile are already present (green)
- Which keywords are missing and need to be added (red)
- Specific advice based on your score

**How ATS works:** Most employers use Applicant Tracking Systems to automatically filter resumes before a human sees them. If your resume doesn't contain the right keywords, it gets rejected automatically — regardless of how qualified you are. This scorer tells you exactly what's missing.

### 📋 AI Prompt Templates
8 ready-to-use prompts that work with **Claude.ai** or **ChatGPT** (both free):
- Resume tailoring — ATS optimized for any specific JD
- Cover letter generator — 4-paragraph, human-sounding
- Application email — under 150 words, professional
- Interview prep — top 10 questions with STAR coaching
- Salary negotiation script — exact scripts for 3 scenarios
- LinkedIn profile optimization — headline, about, skills
- Follow-up email — post-interview within 24 hours
- Career change cover letter — for industry switchers

Copy any prompt → paste into a free AI tool → fill in your details → get a tailored output in seconds.

### 💰 Salary Benchmark Guide
65+ salary ranges across all 16 industries in CAD. Filter by industry. Includes:
- Low to high annual range
- Key role requirements
- Seniority indicators
- Note: always verify with current Glassdoor/LinkedIn Salary data

### 🎤 Interview Prep Guide
- 10 universal questions with coaching notes (click to expand)
- 4 industry-specific questions for all 16 industries
- STAR method framework built in
- What to avoid saying for each question

### 🌐 Job Portal Directory
15 job boards with pro tips for setting up automated alerts:
- Global portals (Indeed, LinkedIn, Glassdoor)
- Canada-specific (Job Bank, Workopolis, OPS, Eluta, Charity Village)
- Industry-specific (Trades, Healthcare, Tech Startups, Staffing)
- How to set up daily email alerts on each one

### 📅 Daily Workflow Guide
A structured 45-minute daily system:
- Step-by-step process from alert emails to submission
- ATS score decision guide (when to apply vs. when to skip)
- Common mistakes that kill applications
- Weekly targets and response rate benchmarks

## The 6-Step Daily Job Search System

This is built into the Workflow tab, but here's the summary:

```
1. Check job alert emails          →  5 min
2. Shortlist 1–2 roles only        →  5 min
3. Run the ATS Scorer              →  3 min
4. Generate tailored resume + CL   → 15 min  (using AI Prompts tab)
5. Read everything out loud        → 10 min
6. Submit and log it               →  5 min
────────────────────────────────────────────
Total                              → ~43 min
```

**Rule:** Never apply to more than 2–3 roles per day. A tailored application beats 20 generic ones every time.

## Known Limitations

- **Salary data is estimated** based on publicly available Canadian market data as of 2025–2026. Always verify with Glassdoor, LinkedIn Salary, or PayScale before negotiating.
- **ATS scoring is approximate** — real ATS systems vary by employer and platform. This tool gives you a strong directional signal, not a guaranteed prediction.
- **AI prompts require a free account** on Claude.ai or ChatGPT — the tool itself has no AI backend.
- **No data is collected** — the tool runs entirely in your browser. Nothing is stored on any server.
